# The Three Ritual Items

Up: [[Plot & Structure]]

**The three dungeon-extracted materials [[Wesley Crane]] needs for [[The Equinox Ritual]] — one from each of the upper Mill-sector floors, each retrieval a cornerstone of [[Wesley's Summer Calendar]].**

> [!danger] DM-only
> These items are the first of the ritual's three lock elements (the others are [[Sam]] as bloodline anchor and the four witness-recruits). Their summer retrieval — and the PCs' chance to deny them — directly sets the [[The Equinox Ritual|Ritual Progress Track]] starting count. See [[Aftermath Branches]].

## The Triad Theme

**Provisional** (bible §20): the dungeon's **record** / the dungeon's **substance** / the dungeon's **lock**. Swap the theme if a better triad serves. Names are also provisional. The **Weight specifically interacts with the Mercer Gate's locking spell as the "lock" component** — it's the dungeon-side material the spell needs to release. The other two contribute to the ritual's accounting in roles still TBD.

## The Items

### Tally Stone — Floor 1 (already retrieved)
A flat stone marked with cuts — the dungeon's **record** of its meals. Wesley extracted it solo ~a week before Session 1. PCs delving Floor 1 in early summer can find the evidence: a small chamber with a fresh recess, the dust-pattern of something just removed, and an active **real sigil** he hasn't returned to clean up (see [[Wesley's Sigils]]).

### Gathered Dust — Floor 2 (late July)
The residue of agency-loss, harvested fresh from a chamber where a civilian has just been hollowed — the dungeon's **substance**. [[Mike and Doug Tinsmith|Mike Tinsmith]]'s late-July hollowing *is* the harvest; Wesley collects the residue while it's fresh. Downstream MOTW: [[Episode 5 — Storm-That-Doesn't-Move]] (residue spillover at Mike's death-site).

### The Weight — Floor 3 (early September, after a failed mid-August attempt)
A small iron disc with a **lock-shape** carved in — the dungeon's **lock**. The first attempt (mid-August) **fails**: Doug Tinsmith is hollowed, Wesley aborts via sigil, no retrieval. The second attempt (early September) **succeeds** with [[Phyllis Harrow]] as the hollowed collateral. See [[The Keystone Theft]] for the Floor 3 keystone Wesley steals around the same window — a separate object from the Weight.

## PCs Can Deny Them

Each item the PCs prevent Wesley from acquiring during the summer lowers the equinox [[The Equinox Ritual|Ritual Progress Track]]'s starting count (3 intact items = track starts 3/5; 0 items ≈ impossible for Wesley to finish in time). Reaching the Weight chamber on Floor 3 *before* Wesley becomes plausible for an aggressive Level-3 party with the longer summer. See [[Where the PCs Can Disrupt]].
