# Plot & Structure

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The antagonist machinery and structural skeleton of Act 1 — **entirely DM-only**. The campaign's prep technique is to write the *bizarro plot* (what [[Wesley Crane]] does if the PCs do nothing) and let each session be the PCs' disruption of it. Underneath the monster-of-the-week foreground, Wesley spends the summer harvesting three ritual items, hollowing adherents, and cultivating recruits toward an autumn-equinox ritual that opens [[The Mercer Gate]]. The PCs piece this together across two "clicks," then cash in a summer of relationship-building at a climactic negotiation-as-combat whose tally decides what kind of rival party walks out of the gate. Failure never ends the campaign — it branches it, on toward the Act 2 race and the Y2K deadline.
