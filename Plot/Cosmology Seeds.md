# Cosmology Seeds

Up: [[Plot & Structure]]

**The tracking table of small Act-1 details that retrospectively become the [[Order and Chaos]] reveal — none exposed to players in Act 1, all accumulating toward the Act 2 explicit reveal.**

> [!danger] DM-only
> In Act 1 the players experience [[The Entity]] as a strange benevolent ally and [[The Dungeon]] as a hungry threat. The deeper symmetry — Order built the dungeon to contain Chaos by draining agency; the entity *is* the contained Chaos — surfaces only gradually. These are the texture beats that do the surfacing. ## Bible Texture Beats (§3, §5)

The reveal these seeds build toward (locked, DM-private): **Order built the dungeon to contain a gathered Chaos-entity** and chose to suppress Chaos locally by **draining choice out of the surrounding population** — the apathy, missing persons, citywide stuckness. The "shitty solution" thesis: Order isn't evil, it built a real solution to a real problem, but the solution is morally bankrupt. **[[The Embers]] are pieces of the contained Chaos** that the entity, sensing the containment field weakening toward 1996, has extruded to cultivate proxies — so the PCs' benevolent patron is the very thing the dungeon was built to contain. The **Y2K deadline** is the field's designed expiration (see [[The Countdown Structure]]). Concrete §3 texture beats to drop: a Filter-broken worker muttering about "the other one, the one that wants us to scream forever"; a Mercer document referencing the dungeon built to contain something the PCs eventually recognize as their entity; the tuning-fork sense growing slightly *too eager* late.
