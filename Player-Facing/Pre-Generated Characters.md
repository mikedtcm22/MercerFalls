# Pre-Generated Characters

Up: [[Player-Facing Materials]]

**Pre-gen character portraits exist for five kids — an option for players who'd rather start from a ready-made Mercer Falls teenager than build from scratch via the [[Character Creation Guide]].**

> [!note] Status: stub — to be designed
> Only the portraits currently exist. Stats, classes, race-as-clique mappings, backgrounds, family situations, and personalities are **to be designed** and are deliberately left loose here. Do not invent details.

## What Exists

Pre-gen portraits are on hand for five named kids:

- **Ren**
- **Frankie**
- **Bash**
- **Zander**
- **Josie**

The portraits live in the Character Creation Guide folder under "Pre-gen Characters Portraits."

## What's TBD

Everything mechanical and characterful: each kid's race-to-clique flavor (see the table in [[Character Creation Guide]]), class, background, the family and friends that make them real, where in town they live (see [[The Districts]]), and why they'd say yes to the dare (see [[Session 1 — The Dare]]). These will be filled in if/when the pre-gens are developed into playable sheets.
