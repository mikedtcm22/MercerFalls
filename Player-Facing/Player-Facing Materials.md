# Player-Facing Materials

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

Everything that's safe to put in front of players: the onboarding documents, the rules references for the three campaign-specific subsystems, the recruitment pitch, and the pre-gen characters. These notes summarize the materials and link to their DM-side counterparts; none of them spoil [[The Bizarro Plot]] or the campaign's cosmology. The throughline is [[The Two Modes of Play]] — living a kid's life topside ([[Time Points]], [[The Negotiation System]]) and crossing the dungeon below ([[Dungeon Mechanics]]).

## Member Notes

- **[[Player Sourcebook]]** — the town-and-tone primer: Mercer Falls in '96, the districts, the people, and the three things that make this campaign play differently.
- **[[Character Creation Guide]]** — building a 1996 kid: race→type-of-kid, classes open, re-flavored backgrounds, the race-to-clique table, "the wrong clique is fine," and races we're not doing.
- **[[Dungeoneering Rules]]** — the 10-minute Stretch system: crawl strategy, running a Stretch, activities, wandering monsters.
- **[[Negotiation Rules Reference]]** — the structured social-encounter one-pager: interest, patience, motivations, pitfalls, and the offer table.
- **[[The Campaign Pitch]]** — the light concept/tone/time-commitment pitch sent with char creation; the title goes unexplained. *(provisional)*
- **[[Pre-Generated Characters]]** — note that pre-gen portraits exist (Ren, Frankie, Bash, Zander, Josie); stats and personalities TBD. *(stub)*
