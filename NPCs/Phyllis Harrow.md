# Phyllis Harrow

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**50s widow, a recent recruit among [[Wesley's Adherents]] — hollowed in the early-September second Weight attempt and dead in the days after.**

## Profile
50s. Widowed. [[The Community Center]] filled a hole for her, and that's how [[Wesley Crane]] drew her in as a recent adherent.

## What happens
**Phyllis is hollowed in the early-September second Weight attempt** (the second pass at extracting the Weight — see [[The Three Ritual Items]]). She dies in the days following — a household accident, kept open for cover-story flavor (bathtub mishap). As with [[Mike and Doug Tinsmith]], the cover story is *accurate*, and [[The Reality Filter]] absorbs the question of why three community-center adults died inside ~6 weeks.

> [!danger] DM-only
> Same investigable signs as the other adherents: a witnessable reconstitution-and-walk-home from [[The Mill]], fading residue in the harvest chamber for a day or two (see [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]]), and an above-ground "accident" whose wrongness is the absence of attention.
