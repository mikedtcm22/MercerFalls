## Function in the campaign
One of the town's cash-conversion channels for dungeon loot (the money loop — see [[Gear and Items]] / [[Time Points]]):
- **Earl's:** fast cash, low rates, no questions ([[Earl]]).
- **The antique dealer:** *slower transactions, better rates — and she asks questions.* That last part is the gameplay friction and the lore hook.
- **[[Russ]] at [[Panels]]:** best rates once rapport is built.

Her **Society auction history** ties her to [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] provenance and to the [[The Mercer Mirror]] (auctioned at some point — relevant to [[Episode 4 — The Mirror]]). Her Heights connections make her a potential bridge to old-money town threads. ([[Russ]]'s back-room collection includes pieces bought at Mercer Society auctions — a likely point of contact.)
