# The Fourth Recruit

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**The fourth seat among [[Wesley's Recruits]] — deliberately undesigned, created by [[Episode 3 — The Click]] rather than existing before it.**

> [!warning] Held loose (TBD)
> Identity, motivations, and pitfalls are **not pre-locked**. They are designed at the moment of [[Episode 3 — The Click]] creation, after the PCs' first two investigations have shaped what kind of recruit they'd plausibly fail to save (or succeed in saving). Do not invent specifics here.

## Function
Featured in [[Episode 3 — The Click]] (the campaign's "first noticing the pattern" beat). Structurally distinct from the other recruits: Episodes 1, 2, and 4 feature *established* Rivals; **Episode 3 *creates* one.** The Fourth Recruit is the episode's "person in danger" or key witness, and the PCs' handling of the episode determines whether this person ends up at [[The Community Center]] at all. Starting disposition 2 (Neutral) by default.

- **PCs handle Episode 3 well:** disposition skews positive; they may never fall fully under [[Wesley Crane]]'s influence; in extreme cases Wesley's ritual is one recruit short by the equinox.
- **PCs fumble Episode 3:** the Fourth Recruit retreats into Wesley's orbit hard; starting disposition for negotiations skews negative.

Their seat is one of the four hollowed-but-conscious witnesses [[The Equinox Ritual]] requires; missing a witness degrades the ritual (see [[Aftermath Branches]], [[Where the PCs Can Disrupt]]).
