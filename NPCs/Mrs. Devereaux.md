# Mrs. Devereaux

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**Late-60s full-time curator of the [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] — sharp, possessive of the collection, and one of only three keyholders to the inner archive where the Mercer papers are sealed.**

## Profile
Late 60s, sharp, **possessive of the collection.** The Society's one full-time curator (over two part-time docents, a Tuesday bookkeeper, and a roster of teen/retiree volunteers — [[Eddie Park]] among them). She has run the chronically underfunded house museum at [[The Mercer Mansion]] since the early '70s.

## Function in the campaign
- **Inner-archive keyholder.** The locked door at the back of the reading room conceals the sealed Mercer papers (see [[Elias Mercer]]). Three people hold keys: Mrs. Devereaux, the board chair, and **[[Wesley Crane]]** (a major donor with two years on the board). Getting PC access to the inner archive — through her, around her, or otherwise — is a real campaign tier.
- **Gatekeeper of access.** [[The Mercer Mansion]] is a primary investigable site; her possessiveness is the social friction the PCs negotiate to reach catalogued correspondence, donation records (Wesley's escalating involvement), and the archive.
- She knows the sealed basement door exists but **assumes it's a disused root cellar** — she has no idea it conceals [[The Mercer Gate]].
