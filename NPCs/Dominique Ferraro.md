# Dominique Ferraro

**[[Bash Whitlock]]'s older cousin (~18) — the people-savvy free spirit who is the social sun three PCs orbit. Bash's one true believer; [[Tank]]'s crush; school-popular and looked up to. The campaign's Chaos-coded counter-example: a kid who moves through the world with genuine ease and warmth.**

*(Formerly "Dom," male, in the pre-gen sheet — now gender-flipped to Dominique, female. "Dom" for short.)*

## Profile
~18, older than the PC kids. She got [[Bash Whitlock]] into computers and is the closest thing he has to a hero — the one person who treats his strangeness as an asset, buys him parts, hears out the 1 a.m. theories, and says *"show me again"* like she half-hopes it's real. But she's also his **exact inverse**: where Bash reads systems and is mystified by people, Dominique *reads people* — she's school-popular, socially fluent, the human world Bash can't access, embodied. That's why he looks up to her, and why losing her would gut him.

## Function
- **The web's hidden hub.** Bash's belief-anchor *and* [[Tank]]'s crush — the gravitational mass that bends Bash and Tank into the same room (and onto the mill prank). See [[The Party]].
- **The party's missing free spirit, in NPC form.** A genuinely free, warm, Chaos-coded foil to a party of cage-carriers — the counter-example, the outside world they're trying to get back to. **Keep her genuinely free, not secretly hollow** — her value is as the counter-example. If complicated later, the richer move is *"her freedom is real and the town/cosmology comes for it anyway,"* never *"she's a fraud."*

> [!danger] DM-only
> **The seeded gut-punch** (from [[Bash Whitlock]]'s arc): his rig opens a precognition window showing **something terrible happening to Dominique** ~two days out — the crisis that forces Bash to refute his own deterministic creed and try to *change* it. Her danger lands precisely because she's a live, loved, on-screen character, not a backstory ghost. Likely **not** present at the dare (older, off-screen) — keep her pristine as the world outside the dungeon until then.
