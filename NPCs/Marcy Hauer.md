# Marcy Hauer

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**16, the most public of [[Wesley's Recruits]] — front-desk fixture at [[The Community Center]], talented artist, grief-shaped, and reachable through her dead brother's memory.**

## Profile
16. Brother **Tommy** died in [[The 1991 Mill Incident]] when she was 6; her grandmother (and guardian) died in 1994 — [[Wesley Crane]] gave that eulogy. She's been a community-center fixture for two years: runs the front desk, attends every event, introduces Wesley to people. Talented at drawing; her art is how she processes what she can't say.

## Arc / function
Featured in [[Episode 2 — The Hungry House]]. Her connection to [[Henry Voss]]'s circle (via Tommy's 1991 work-friends) makes her a peripheral but emotionally important figure there — she still checks on Voss, brings flowers on the anniversary. A PC following the Voss thread meets her in that routine; that encounter sets her disposition. Deep engagement through Tommy's memory shifts her to Sympathetic/Connected; brusqueness or treating her visits as suspicious drops her to Wary. After Episode 2, [[Wesley Crane]] begins counter-cultivating her.

> [!danger] DM-only
> **Negotiation profile (provisional — finalized after PC creation).** Motivations: Legacy (Tommy & her grandmother), Benevolence (the center work feels like real charity), Discovery (her art). Pitfalls: Power (being condescended to / "you're just a kid"), Greed (transactional approaches read as cynical adult bullshit). Reachable through: Tommy specifically, the grandmother, the realization that some she's "helping" are people Wesley is hollowing, her art as something outside Wesley's framework. **Wesley's counter-cultivation** (begins after Ep2): reframes PC kindness as condescension — "*she treats you like a kid — she doesn't see what we see.*" Her hollowing across the summer is *instrumental* to [[The Equinox Ritual]] (a hollowed-but-conscious witness). See [[The Negotiation System]] for equinox resolution.
