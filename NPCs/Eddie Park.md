# Eddie Park

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**15, lonely and brilliant, haunting the library annex inside [[The Mercer Mansion]] — and load-bearing for [[Wesley Crane]] because he is [[Sam]]'s cousin and unknowingly adjacent to Mercer's sealed papers.**

## Profile
15. Mom died of cancer three years ago; dad emotionally absent. Eddie is on the [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] volunteer roster and effectively haunts the library-annex reading room inside [[The Mercer Mansion]]. [[Wesley Crane]] has been talking with him since spring. He connects Wesley to [[Sam]] (his older cousin) — load-bearing in ways Wesley appreciates and Eddie doesn't yet understand.

## Arc / function
Featured in [[Episode 4 — The Mirror]] (the Mercer-faint). The library annex is a [[Time Points|Time-Points]]-investigable site, so PCs going there meet Eddie naturally. **Important wrinkle:** the annex's locked inner archive is also where Mercer's papers are sealed — Eddie is unknowingly adjacent to one of the campaign's most important late-game artifacts (the documents that eventually break [[Wesley Crane]]; see [[The Mercer Mansion]]). The hook is intellect and recognition — being seen. After Episode 4, [[Wesley Crane]] begins counter-cultivating him.

> [!danger] DM-only
> **Negotiation profile (provisional).** Motivations: Discovery (the biggest lever — he wants to know), Legacy (his mother, his cousin [[Sam]]), Higher Authority (still seeking a worthy one). Pitfalls: Power (he doesn't want to lead; pressure to step up overwhelms him), Revelry (he's past being treated as a kid). Reachable through: treating him as a real intellectual peer, showing him Mercer-papers material Wesley has hidden, telling him what's actually happening to Sam. **Wesley's counter-cultivation** (after Ep4): any PC who used Eddie as a research-source without forming a real bond gives Wesley a clean attack — "*they just needed the library access, Eddie — they don't actually want to know you.*" His hollowing is *instrumental* to [[The Equinox Ritual]]. See [[The Negotiation System]].
