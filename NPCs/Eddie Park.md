Originally placed on the [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] volunteer roster, effectively haunting the library-annex reading room inside [[The Mercer Mansion]]. ## Arc / function (provisional)
Formerly featured in [[Episode 4 — The Mirror]] (the Mercer-faint). **Important wrinkle that may survive the rebuild:** the annex's locked inner archive is where Mercer's papers are sealed — so a library-adjacent Eddie would be unknowingly next to one of the campaign's most important late-game artifacts (the documents that eventually break [[Wesley Crane]]; see [[The Mercer Mansion]]). Whether the rock-bottom-Paladin redesign keeps that library tie is a deep-dive question. The hook is recognition — being seen.

> [!danger] DM-only — negotiation profile (provisional, pre-rebuild)

Pitfalls: Power (he doesn't want to lead), Revelry (past being treated as a kid). Reachable through: treating him as a real peer rather than a convert. His hollowing is the *qualifier* ("the Gathering Hollows") for [[The Equinox Ritual]]. See [[The Negotiation System]], [[Wesley's Recruits]].
