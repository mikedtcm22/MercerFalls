# Henry Voss

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**A hollowed 1991 mill survivor who shrugs through his days behind the [[The Sinclair Station]] counter — and whose house, fed by dungeon-stone he doesn't remember carrying home, is becoming [[The Hungry House]].**

## Profile
A survivor of [[The 1991 Mill Incident]], hollowed five years ago and never recovered. Works the [[The Sinclair Station]] counter — the campaign's walking proof of agency-loss. The embers' fire vocabulary, reversed, describes him: *no warmth, the ember gone out, ash behind his eyes, a stove banked too long.* He isn't lying when he says he doesn't remember things — he genuinely doesn't. See [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]].

## Arc / function ([[Episode 2 — The Hungry House]])
After the 1991 incident, Voss walked home with a piece of dungeon-stone in his lunchbox — an artifact he doesn't remember picking up. The stone has slowly made his house an annex of the dungeon; a small chamber has formed in his basement and is growing. That house is **[[The Hungry House]]** on Pine Street: pets vanish near the front yard, then a kid on a bike, then the mailman (who turns up confused two days later). PCs resolve it by destroying or extracting the dungeon-stone (the basement chamber holds a small Floor-1-style native or two).

[[Marcy Hauer]] still checks on Voss — her brother Tommy was his friend before 1991 — which is the disposition-setting encounter for her episode. Earl ([[Earl|Earl's Pawn Shop]] owner) can note Voss is one of dozens of locals on a slow pawn-drip, the first whisper of the hollowed *pattern*.

> [!danger] DM-only
> **Wesley fingerprint: none** — Voss is independent of [[Wesley Crane]]'s operations; the episode plants the 1991-survivor thread. Voss is hard to Filter-pierce at Level 1 and probably won't be reached here; if Filter-broken later he'd say something Order-flavored. PCs may notice his slow drawl matches other townspeople — the first hint that 1991 wasn't an isolated accident. (One of the 2–3 hollowed mill workers named per bible §20 item 18; Voss at the Sinclair is locked.)
