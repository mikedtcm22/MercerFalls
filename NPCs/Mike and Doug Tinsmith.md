# Mike and Doug Tinsmith

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**Mid-50s brothers, longtime [[The Community Center]] volunteers and two of [[Wesley's Adherents]] — both hollowed during ritual-item harvests, both dead within days in cover-story "accidents."**

## Profile
Brothers, mid-50s. Longtime community-center volunteers, fully inside [[Wesley Crane]]'s orbit. Unlike [[Wesley's Recruits]], adherents are adults whose hollowing is direct and lethal — Wesley spends them to harvest [[The Three Ritual Items]].

## What happens
- **Mike** is hollowed in the **late-July Gathered Dust harvest**. He drowns four days later on a routine fishing trip Wesley casually suggested — at [[The Fishing Dock]] / the lake stretch he'd fished for thirty years and "had no business not seeing the current" that killed him.
- **Doug**, present at the harvest but not hollowed there, is *more* committed afterward. He is hollowed in the **mid-August first Weight attempt** and dies a few days later in a household accident (a ladder fall).
- **Cover stories are accurate** — Mike really drowned, Doug really fell off a ladder. [[The Reality Filter]] handles the question of *why* multiple community-center adults died inside ~6 weeks.
