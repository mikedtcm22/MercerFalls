# Wendell Pace

Up: [[Cast & NPCs]]

**The janitor [[Wesley Crane|Wesley]] dragged into the dungeon as a hollowing victim — the man the PCs watch die and dissolve under the juvenile [[The Hush|Hush]] in the [[The Pillared Reach|Pillared Reach]]. He turns up alive and hollowed topside: the campaign's first proof that "death in the dungeon isn't exactly death."**

## Profile
Middle-aged town janitor; unremarkable, the kind of man a town doesn't miss quickly. Wesley brought him down as labor (and a hollowing seed). In [[Session 1 — The Dare|Session 1]] the PCs come on him in the [[The Pillared Reach|Pillared Reach]], where the juvenile Hush is killing him — the blood trail across the [[The Spiked Corridor|Spiked Corridor]] and [[The Buzzsaw Room|Buzzsaw Room]] is his. The party watches him **die and dissolve.**

## Function: "death in the dungeon isn't death"
Per the dungeon's death mechanic ([[Death, TPK, and the Lost Day]]), a body that dies below **reconstitutes at the entrance and walks home on autopilot.** So Wendell is **alive** afterward — and, because he didn't leave free-willed, **hollowed:** going through the motions of his routine, flat and *off.* An **Insight** check catches the wrongness (lights on, nobody home); no dramatic reveal, just a quiet, unsettling *"...wasn't that guy—?"*

He's a **seed**, not a plot node: if no PC actually "died" in Session 1, a living-but-hollowed Wendell is the backstop that plants *dying below isn't final* before the players need to understand it. Incidental, never required.

## Where found
Wherever the kids would recognize him. Efficient placement: **janitoring [[The Mercer Mansion]]** — a town-history trip then plants both the cordial [[Wesley Crane|Wesley]] and his hollowed victim in one room, connected by neither (see [[Episode 2 — The Hush — Town-Time Investigation Map]]). Alternatives: a [[Marco's Diner]] regular, or shuffling the Flats near [[Jerry's Pawn Shop]].

> [!danger] DM-only
> Wendell is **Wesley's** victim (Wesley brought him down) — a link the players cannot make yet and shouldn't. Hold it for the [[Wesley Crane]] recognition arc. His hollowing also quietly feeds [[Jerry]]'s "who's been acting strange" whisper-count and [[Jerry's Pawn Shop|the pawn shop]]'s hollowed-count ledger.
