# Game Systems

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The mechanical engine of the campaign — how play actually runs at the table. Everything hangs off **[[The Two Modes of Play]]**: a Persona-like Town Mode metered by [[Time Points]] and built toward [[The Negotiation System]]'s equinox climax, versus a roguelike Dungeon Mode of attrition, hollowing, and forensic investigation. Several systems are player-usable as written; a handful **encode the campaign's secrets** — the Floor Intelligence Gradient is really Wesley, the sigils are his anti-ember relic, and the Surfeit counter quietly ties the PCs' deaths to his rising power. DM-only and mixed notes flag their secret parts inline.

## The Notes

- [[The Two Modes of Play]] — Town Mode (Persona, Time Points, permanent death) vs. Dungeon Mode (roguelike, attrition, hollowing). **(player-facing)**
- [[Time Points]] — daily Day/Night pools, threshold pricing, delves cost the whole day, location-introduction rule, the Money Loop. **(player-facing)**
- [[The Negotiation System]] — Coville/Draw Steel framework, the gradual reveal, the **Disposition Tracker**, and the equinox negotiation-as-combat. **(mixed)**
- [[Dungeon Mechanics]] — Stretches, attrition, the 5 encounter types, fast-combat (2d10), flee rule, milestone leveling, speedrunning, retreating. **(mixed)**
- [[Floor Reset and the Intelligence Gradient]] — daily reset (chalk-and-glow persists), and the depth gradient that is really Wesley + the Wesley-status signal. **(dm-only)**
- [[Forensic Delving]] — reading floor history through residue, sigils, chalk, and glyphs; the campaign's signature investigation skill. **(mixed)**
- [[Death, TPK, and the Lost Day]] — what costs what, the dissolve, the walk home, and the lost day narrated at the table. **(mixed)**
- [[The Keystone Tablet]] — map + compass slots, per-floor keystones, the player map handouts, and the persistent Atlas notes. **(mixed)**
- [[Wesley's Sigils]] — real + decoy sigils, detection, leveling, banked abilities, anti-embers, and the Surfeit counter. **(dm-only)**
- [[Gear and Items]] — stock-5e weapons/armor, the dungeon as source, starter gear via the Awakening, the stash, magic items, identifying. **(mixed)**

## Items
- [[The Stone of Quietude]] — a *Silence* magic item (stone-centered, throwable, 3 charges / 1d4 per long rest); the optional Episode-2 prize against the sound-hunting [[The Hush|Hush]]. **(mixed)**
- [[The Hush-Hunting Graphic Novel]] — the den-tracking lore item [[Russ]] routes the PCs to (the [[The Library|Library]] or [[Jerry's Pawn Shop|Jerry's]]) when he can't find the lair himself. **(mixed)**
