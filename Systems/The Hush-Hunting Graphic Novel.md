# The Hush-Hunting Graphic Novel

Up: [[Gear and Items]]

**An obscure graphic-novelization of the same B-movie [[Russ]] IDs the Hush from — the one that expands the lore and shows the heroes taking the fight to the creature. It's how the PCs learn to *hunt* a [[The Hush|Hush]] to its lair, the one thing Russ himself can't give them.**

## What it is
A slim, out-of-print comic adaptation that goes further than the film: in it the characters stop running and go on the offensive, and the book lays out — in that lurid, over-explained tie-in way — **how you track a Hush to its den.** Russ knows the creature's *habits* (that it takes people and children, ripens live prey, hunts by sound); he does **not** know how to find a *nest.* For that he points the kids here.

## In play (Episode 2)
- **Payload:** the **den-tracking method** (F4) — turns a rough directional bearing from the [[The Mill|mill grounds]] / the nightly stalk into an actual hunt, so the party can locate the [[The Hush|Hush]]'s den (supports C5) and take the fight to it in the climax.
- **Also:** a little expanded, unreliable lore color — useful, but a *comic,* so the DM decides what's accurate.
