# Bestiary

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The campaign's monsters — each one curated by [[The Nightmare Engine]] from the fears and pop-culture circulating through [[Mercer Falls, Ohio]]: a B-movie creature, a cursed family heirloom, a wrong weather-front. Every entry works backward from a **cultural source** with a stated **vulnerability** the PCs can research (usually through [[Russ]] at [[Panels]]) and a **secondary tell** that bridges the encounter to the research thread. This MOC holds the named monsters that anchor episodes; generic dungeon-natives are handled at the table via [[Dungeon Mechanics]]. Most entries are early-pass `provisional`/`stub` — designed 1–2 episodes ahead.

## Member notes
- **[[The Hush]]** — eyeless, sound-hunting B-movie predator; the [[Episode 1 — The Hush|Episode 1]] antagonist (~25 HP, +5, 1d8+2; advantage on sound, staggered by split sound; killed via misdirection ± silence). Wolf-front, light-eating hide, never flanked; juvenile / adult forms; source film TBD. *(Renamed from "The Watcher.")*
- **[[The Mercer Mirror]]** — a cursed Mercer-family mirror at [[Panels]] that reflects what isn't there; an Order-touched echo predating Wesley; [[Episode 4 — The Mirror|Episode 4]].
- **[[The Storm-That-Doesn't-Move]]** — a stationary residue-storm over Mike Tinsmith's death-site that hollows fish; spillover from the Floor-2 harvest; [[Episode 5 — Storm-That-Doesn't-Move|Episode 5]].
