# The Dogmen

Up: [[Bestiary]]

**Floor 1's pack predators — human-scaled, upright beast-men with dogs' heads: the half-believed "dogman" of Midwest campfire legend, given a body. A [[The Nightmare Engine|reskin]] of the gnoll family — D&D stat blocks, Mercer Falls flavor.**

## Concept & source
A [[The Nightmare Engine|Nightmare Engine]] creature, curated from the **Midwest "dogman" legend** (late-night radio, campfire stories) a 1996 rural-Ohio kid would half-believe. They are the organized core of Floor 1's **"pack floor"** lair theme (see [[The Mill Sector]]): dogmen hold the floor as a hunting range, with unaligned feral beasts (wolves, hyenas, death dogs) roaming around them. **Beast, not demon** — none of the Abyssal / Yeenoghu flavor of book gnolls.

## What they look like (locked)
- **Human-scaled, upright.** At a glance you might take one for a man — until the shapes stop adding up. The wrongness is the **human/dog clash**, not bulk.
- **Head:** a long dog/hyena muzzle, heavy jaw, small flattened ears, too many teeth in a lolling grin; animal eyes that catch light underground.
- **Frame:** an almost-human upright body, but the **arms hang too long**, ending in **scarred, half-human/half-paw hands** — dexterous enough to grip crude scavenged blades and pry a crate. Digitigrade legs; can drop to all fours and lope when it runs (a fast marauder).
- **Coat/condition:** short, mangy, patchy fur — mange-grey and dirty tan, worn to bare grey skin in places; scarred; a carrion-and-wet-dog reek. Feral; scavenged straps and bone/scrap weapons, no uniform.

## Size = rank
Height and bulk signal status in the pack:
- **Rank-and-file:** just under **6 ft** — reads as a wrong-looking man.
- **Lieutenant:** ~**6½ ft** (the modified Pack Lord in [[The Buzzsaw Room]]).
- **Chief / Pack Lord:** the full ~**7 ft**, met deeper/later.

## Mechanics (reskin chassis)
Use the SRD/MM **Gnoll** family as the chassis, dressed as dogmen:
- **Rank-and-file:** Gnoll (CR 1/2) — crude blades; the pack-floor wandering-table core.
- **Lieutenant:** modified **Gnoll Pack Lord** — warrior Str/Dex, keeps **Multiattack** and a **reach (whip) weapon**, **no Incite Rampage** (see [[The Buzzsaw Room]]).
- **Chief:** Gnoll Pack Lord as-printed (CR 2) or heavier, for a later/deeper set-piece.
- Ambient pack-floor company (not dogmen): the Pit Room (9) wolves, Giant Hyena, death dogs, and one stronger beast the dogmen fear (TBD).

## First appearance
Two **dead** dogmen lie in [[The Octagonal Vestibule]] in Session 1 — Wesley's clean pre-clear kills, lootable (native-death rule; see [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]]) — the party's first look at the creature before they fight a live one (the guaranteed wandering roll in [[The Expedition Post]]).
