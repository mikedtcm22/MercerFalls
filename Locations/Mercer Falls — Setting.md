# Mercer Falls — Setting

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The places of *Embers of the Gathering Hollows*: the rust-belt lake town of [[Mercer Falls, Ohio]] in summer 1996, its geography, institutions, and the layers of wrongness that sit on top of ordinary small-town life — plus the dungeon spaces hidden beneath it. The town's spatial spine runs north-to-south: [[Mercer Lake]] at the top, draining through [[The River and the Falls]] (enclosed through [[The Mill]] atop the bluff) down to downtown. Beneath the surface, [[The Dungeon]] occupies a half-mile circle with three entrances. Most town locations read **real first, wrong second**: a player-visible surface with a DM-only hidden layer (flagged inline). Dungeon spaces and [[The Mercer Gate]] lean fully DM-only. For who lives and works here see [[Cast & NPCs]] and [[Factions & Groups]]; for what's beneath see [[Lore & Cosmology]].

## Town & Geography
- [[Mercer Falls, Ohio]] — ~18k rust-belt lake town; the geographic spine and civic overview.
- [[The Mill]] — Mercer Steel Works, sealed since 1991, sitting atop the dungeon's south entrance.
- [[Mercer Lake]] — the daylight social engine; dual-pole (beach vs. cold spot).
- [[The River and the Falls]] — the enclosed river, the 50-foot cascade, the overlook, civic identity.
- [[The Mercer Mansion]] — Historical Society HQ; library annex, inner archive, sealed basement door.
- [[The Districts]] — Heights, Flats, Sycamore, Downtown; a quiet map of who the hollowing reached.
- [[The Wrongness Layer]] — five layers of ambient, behavioral, and material wrongness.

## Establishments
- [[Panels]] — Russ's comic shop, back-room curio collection, monster-research hub.
- [[Marco's Diner]] — downtown diner and default town-mode hangout.
- [[The Community Center]] — Wesley's warm front, with a sigil-paired private back room.
- [[Earl's Pawn Shop]] — the Flats pawn shop; the accidental witness with a hollowed-count ledger.
- [[The Sinclair Station]] — gas station where the hollowed Henry Voss works the counter.
- [[The Hungry House]] — Voss's Pine Street house turning into a dungeon annex (Episode 2).
- [[The Fishing Dock]] — Mike Tinsmith's death site; a river cold spot (Episode 5).

## Dungeon Spaces
- [[The Mercer Gate]] — the sealed mansion-basement entrance; three-element lock; Act-2 launch.
- [[The Dungeon's Surface Footprint]] — the half-mile circle, three entrances, fragmented-arc sectors.
- [[The Ember Chamber]] — the adjacent pocket space that opens once for the awakening.
- [[The Mill Sector]] — the Act-1 dungeon: floors 1-5, Floor 1's donut layout, the Buzzsaw Room.
