# The Mill Sector

Up: [[Mercer Falls — Setting]]

**The Act-1 dungeon: floors 1-5 of [[The Dungeon]] reached through [[The Mill]]'s lower works — the campaign's heaviest dungeon-design investment, dozens of sessions of grinding, glyph-decoding, and cat-and-mouse with [[Wesley Crane]].**

> [!danger] DM-only
> This entire note is a spoiler. The Mill sector is the only entrance the PCs can use through Act 1 (via trespass into the sealed mill grounds).

## Where it sits
One of three fragmented-arc sectors of the dungeon's upper floors (see [[The Dungeon's Surface Footprint]]). The Mill, Mercer, and Lake sectors' floors 1-5 **do not connect**; the system unifies only at floor 6. PCs entering from the mill descend through industrial space, then the bluff's bedrock, then dungeon proper. **Floors 1-5 are heavy content by design** — this is where the Act-1 investment goes. (By contrast the **Mercer sector** is a low-detail transit corridor used in Act 2, and the **Lake entrance** drops straight to a deep Tier-2 floor — both noted here only for orientation; see [[The Dungeon's Surface Footprint]] for full detail.)

## Floor 1 — layout (closed at the layout level)
**Sixteen rooms in a donut footprint**, with the Mercer/Lake sectors forming an unmapped "bite" out of the ring. Three branches fan from the central **Octagonal Vestibule**. The wrapping **Stutter Stair** connects the Wesley tangent to the Counting Room; a **one-way painted door** runs from the Map-Keystone Niche to the Bell Junction. Two rooms — the **Down-Stairs Chamber** and the **Buzzsaw Room** — are deliberately placed against the bite walls as future hidden-shortcut entrances for when the Mercer and Lake sectors come online.

Named/known rooms:
- **Octagonal Vestibule** — the central hub; three branches fan out.
- **Stutter Stair** — wrapping stair; a hidden landing on its descent leads to the Compass Cradle.
- **Map-Keystone Niche (Room 6)** — holds the **Map-Keystone** behind a glyph-lock (puzzle specifics TBD).
- **Compass Cradle (Room 16)** — holds the **Compass-Keystone**, reached via the Stutter Stair's hidden landing; guarded by a "deep guardian" (TBD).
- **Bell Junction** — destination of the one-way painted door.
- **Counting Room** / **Down-Stairs Chamber** — the latter against a bite wall.
- **Pillared Reach** — where the young [[The Hush]] is encountered.
- **Buzzsaw Room** — the Session-1 climax chamber (see below); against a bite wall.

See [[The Keystone Tablet]] for what the two Floor-1 keystones do.

## Floor 1 in Session 1
The floor is **partially pre-cleared by Wesley** on a recent solo delve; the party follows his footprints, spent torches, drag marks, a cloth scrap (seen again on Wesley), stacked corpses, tally marks, and chalk-struck glyphs — their first encounter with the glyph-language. The trail ends at the **Buzzsaw Room**, where Wesley is finalizing **his first real sigil** ([[Wesley's Sigils]]); he says one line ("You poor things"), triggers the sigil, and vanishes. The engineered wave that follows is tuned for a likely TPK with a narrow sigil-escape window. See [[Session 1 — The Dare]].

## Floors 2-5 and steady-state mechanics
Floors 2-5 await detailed design (built one at a time as each comes online). Steady-state: daily intrinsic **floor reset** (chalk and glow persist; footprints and blood are gone), Stretch-based resource attrition, the glyph-language as a persistent knowledge resource, and the **sigil cat-and-mouse** with Wesley (real vs. decoy sigils). Forensic delving — residue-reading, sigil-reading, tracking Wesley's status via the gradient-shuffle — is the signature mechanic here. See [[Dungeon Mechanics]], [[Floor Reset and the Intelligence Gradient]], [[Forensic Delving]].

The Act-1 climax is partial access to **floor 6**, repulsed before significant exploration. After [[The Mercer Gate]] opens at the equinox, the Mill sector's floors 1-5 stop being campaign-active (explored, mapped, drained).
