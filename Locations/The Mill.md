# The Mill

Up: [[Mercer Falls — Setting]]

**Mercer Steel Works, sealed since the 1991 incident — the town's center of gravity, sitting directly atop the dungeon's main south entrance.**

## Description
A long, narrow steel complex (~400 ft north-south, ~150 ft wide) built into the mill bluff's bedrock in 1887, oriented along the river that runs **enclosed** through its full length. The river enters at the north end through controlled intakes (powering turbines, cooling, slag quenching, boiler feed) and exits at the south end, dropping over the bluff's south edge as [[The River and the Falls|the falls]]. Internal layout: rolling mill, foundry, administration block, outbuildings, a covered loading area on an old rail spur. The **blast-furnace stack** is the tallest structure in town. Chain-link perimeter, padlocked gates, weeds. The river is visible *outside* the mill (north, and south as the falls) but not visible *inside* the grounds — it's enclosed in industrial channels throughout.

The mill commands views in both directions — north to the river, lake, and [[The Mercer Mansion|Bluff]]; south to the falls, downtown, and the cemetery. Mercer chose its location partly for this.

## In play
Sealed since 1991. The **south gate** faces the road to downtown; the main investigable approach is by trespass into the sealed grounds (this is the Session-1 dare site — see [[Session 1 — The Dare]]). After Session 1 the mill becomes a Time-Points-investigable location: the administration offices hold payroll records, photo files, the redacted 1991 incident report, and possibly a small Wesley shrine.

> [!danger] DM-only
> The mill was built over the dungeon's known surface entrance. Its lower works (basement and sub-basement) extend into the bluff's bedrock and are *physically continuous* with the dungeon's upper levels — there is no clear line where "industrial basement" stops and "something older" begins. PCs entering from the mill descend through industrial space, then bedrock, then [[The Mill Sector]] proper. The **[[The 1991 Mill Incident|1991 incident]]** was workers in the deeper sections breaking through into the dungeon and being hollowed (see [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]]); official cause was a gas leak, seven dead. Hollowed survivors still in town include [[Henry Voss]]. **Dungeon-stone is set into the keystone above the south gate** — visible to anyone who looks up, noticed by no one (see [[The Wrongness Layer]]). Hollowed workers' walks home come out through this gate. Perpetual residue accumulates here once PCs can perceive residue (mid-Act 2).
