# The Community Center

Up: [[Mercer Falls — Setting]]

**[[Wesley Crane]]'s "spiritual community center" in downtown — a genuinely warm front-of-house and, behind it, the sigil-paired private room that anchors his dungeon operations.**

## Description
A downtown storefront-and-apartment, opened in 1996, with Wesley living in the apartment above. The public face is exactly what it appears: grief-support groups, Sunday sermons, events, a front desk. Wesley is as charming here as ever — most of his summer is low-key community work, and the PCs may not see him for whole stretches. Several [[Wesley's Recruits|recruits]] become increasingly visible here across the summer: **Marcy runs the front desk**, **Ricky** quits his summer job by August to help full-time, and Eddie and the Fourth Recruit drift in. The recruits and [[Wesley's Adherents|adherents]] becoming a visible *group* here is part of the [[The Pattern Recognition Arc|pattern recognition arc]].

## In play
A canonical town-investigation venue ("a community center drop-in" is a standard Time-Points action). The center is where **Wesley's name first becomes legibly connected to a death** — [[Mike and Doug Tinsmith|Mike Tinsmith]] was a volunteer here (Episode 5), as were Doug and [[Henry Voss]] (grief support). By Episode 6-8 the accumulation of community-center connections drives the second "click": *it's that guy at the community center.* PCs with strong bonds to Marcy or Ricky may gain inside access — which Wesley counter-cultivates against.

> [!danger] DM-only
> The **back room is a locked-to-public private space** and Wesley's **sigil-paired re-entry site** — the above-ground anchor from which he re-enters his dungeon sigils once (see [[Wesley's Sigils]]). It is also where his community-center work **processes dungeon materials into ritual-ready form** for [[The Mercer Gate]]'s lock (see [[The Three Ritual Items]], [[The Equinox Ritual]]). It is the **leading candidate for where Wesley keeps the stolen Floor 3 map keystone** after [[The Keystone Theft]] (he may later transfer it to [[The Mercer Mansion]] once he has mansion access). PCs reach the back room only via confrontation or a stealth/social incursion.
