# The Hungry House

Up: [[Mercer Falls — Setting]]

**[[Henry Voss]]'s house on Pine Street in [[The Districts|The Flats]] — slowly turning into an annex of the dungeon, and the monster site of [[Episode 2 — The Hungry House]].**

## Description
A small frame house on Pine Street, owned by **[[Henry Voss]]**, a 1991 mill survivor hollowed for five years. After [[The 1991 Mill Incident|the 1991 incident]] he walked home with a piece of **dungeon-stone in his lunchbox** — an artifact he doesn't remember picking up. The stone has been slowly making the house a dungeon annex: a small chamber has formed in the basement and is growing, with one or two small dungeon-natives inside (similar in style to what PCs see on [[The Mill Sector|Floor 1]]).

## In play (Episode 2, late June)
**The monster:** the house has begun *feeding.* Pets disappear near the front yard; then a kid on a bike; then the mailman, who turns up confused two days later with no memory of the morning. **Town investigation:** neighbors, missing-pet posters, the mailman's account, and Voss's hollowed affect (he genuinely doesn't remember). The thread introduces [[Earl's Pawn Shop]] (Voss has been pawning to make mortgage payments) and **[[Marcy Hauer]]**, whose brother Tommy was Voss's pre-1991 work-friend — Marcy still checks on Voss, and a PC who follows the thread meets her here. This is Marcy's disposition-setting beat (she starts Neutral).

**Resolution:** a pure town episode — the chamber is in the basement. PCs either **destroy the dungeon-stone** (closing the annex) or **extract it** (causing localized residue dispersal). Either works.

> [!danger] DM-only
> The Hungry House is the campaign's clearest small-scale demonstration that **the dungeon leaks itself into the world** as material, not just monsters — the dungeon-stone is a node of [[The Dungeon's Surface Footprint]] reach beyond the circle. It plants the **1991-survivor thread** and the dungeon-stone "click" clue ([[The Pattern Recognition Arc]], [[Episode 3 — The Click]]). Wesley fingerprint: **none** — this is older than him. Voss is one of the [[Hollowed Townspeople]] ([[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]], [[The Reality Filter]]).
