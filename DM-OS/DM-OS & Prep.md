# DM-OS & Prep

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The "how we run it" layer of the campaign: the operating system for prepping, running, capturing, and improving a long-form weekly game, plus the live prep backlog and open design questions. Where the campaign bible and [[The Bizarro Plot]] are the *campaign architecture*, these notes are the *meta-architecture* — the system that lets the campaign be run well, observed accurately, and changed fast when it isn't working. The pipeline runs on Discord + Archivist AI + Avrae, organized around one keystone idea: every recurring DM process must produce a player-facing artifact.
