# Bash Whitlock

Rock Gnome Artificer · 13 (skipped a grade) · smooth through a screen, lost face-to-face. Ideal: *it's all readable — you just need the right instrument.* Flaw: *anything glowing beats anyone talking.* Bond: his cousin [[Dominique Ferraro]].

## The read — Order/Chaos in his epistemology
He can't be conned by words (skeptical to the bone, "assumes every system has a backdoor") — so [[The Entity]] cons him with **data**: a working instrument, a confirmed prediction, a readout that's *right*. His precognition "window" and his gear picking up the mill's electrical hash are, unknowingly, an early antenna for the wrongness — **the window is the Entity.** Chasing it is, unknowingly, serving the thing under the town.
- **[[Order and Chaos|Order]] tempts his worldview:** total readability = a determined, agency-dead clockwork (the sealed box).
- **Chaos tempts his appetite:** the lit-up chase; "the people slowing you down are the cage."
- **Redemption:** the unreadable — a person, a free choice, a changeable future — isn't a gap in his instrument; it's the *signature* of the agency the whole cosmology is about.

## Awakening test — "The Basement: the Box and the Rig" (locked)
His station is his own basement, recreated. Two devices on opposite walls; touch one, the other dissolves (mote-fade). **The black box:** sealed, impenetrable, *works* — shows the near future, one slider, no way in (the one system he can't open). **The open rig:** wires bare, alive, throwing static — and a voice (maybe [[Dominique Ferraro|Dom]]) surfacing and dropping. Post-it (survives the choice): *"one of these works. / the other just wants to talk. / you already know which one you've been chasing — go."*
- **Rig branch:** ember wedged in the machinery (he must *work* it free); the static clears to Dom's voice — *"…you were right, man, you were always—"* (cut off).
- **Box branch:** the screen shows him finding the ember behind an **air vent** (the next few seconds); he enacts it; the box goes inert (miracle spent).
Aligned = the rig (the unreadable, the person). The tell: it nudged him (post-it) toward a *sealed system he could never open* and asked him to dissolve a voice that might be Dom's. See [[The Ember Chamber]], [[The Embers]].

## Battle plan
- **Through-line — The Corrupted Instrument.** His gear is the Entity's antenna, feeding true-seeming readouts that nudge him and (since he's the party's research engine) the whole party. The recurring shape: **sealed answers vs. open, human paths** — trusting the sealed one serves the thing under the town.
- **Early/mid lean — Glowing Beats Talking.** Reward the machine-over-person reflex, then make it cost something human (heads-down when the team needed him; a clue followed off a cliff).
- **Crisis & turn — Dom on the Screen.** The rig opens a window on [[Dominique Ferraro]] in danger. If the future is fixed/readable (his creed), she's doomed — and the Awakening fragment detonates (being *right* about a determined world is what dooms her). His turn: change a future a screen showed him → assert agency, refute his own creed for love.
- **Reserve:** the precognition window (DM-controlled — true warning, manipulation, or red herring); the long-game reveal that his materialist certainty was the Entity's bait.

## Relationships
Knows [[Gern]] via mother-arranged playdates (assigned, not chosen). Connected to [[Tank]] through [[Dominique Ferraro]] — Bash recruits Tank as a protector and to **phreak him a free second phone line**. At the mill on a **dare** — really to ingratiate himself with Tank. Deliberate foil to [[Old Man]]: the skeptic who distrusts the beautiful line beside the face who fools everyone with it. See [[The Party]].
