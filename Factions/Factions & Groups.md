# Factions & Groups

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The people of Mercer Falls sort into a handful of standing groups, and the campaign's central conflict is largely a contest between them. On one side sit the kids of [[The Party]], the only townsfolk who can both see the dungeon's work and act on it. Against them, [[Wesley Crane]] has assembled two human instruments — the kids of [[Wesley's Recruits]], whom he needs as ritual witnesses, and the older [[Wesley's Adherents]], whom he hollows for ingredients. At the equinox, the recruits crystallize into [[The Other Party (Rival or Evil)]], whose alignment the PCs determine by negotiation. Around all of this stand the town's institutions and victims: the [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] that unknowingly shelters Wesley's project, the old occult lineage of [[The Mercer Gentleman's Club]], and the drifting ranks of the [[Hollowed Townspeople]] — walking evidence nobody else can read.

## Member notes
- [[The Party]] — the PCs: dungeon-touched, ember-bearing kids; not fated, just eligible (6–12 candidates) and present at the right moment.
- [[Wesley's Recruits]] — Marcy, Ricky, Eddie, and the Fourth; the four kids Wesley cultivates as ritual witnesses. *(DM-only.)*
- [[Wesley's Adherents]] — Mike & Doug Tinsmith and Phyllis Harrow; older volunteers hollowed instrumentally to harvest ritual ingredients. *(DM-only.)*
- [[The Other Party (Rival or Evil)]] — the parallel NPC ember-party that emerges at the equinox; Rival, Evil, or Compromised per negotiation. *(DM-only.)*
- [[Mercer Falls Historical Society]] — runs the mansion museum; Mrs. Devereaux curates; Wesley a board donor for cover; three keys to the inner archive.
- [[The Mercer Gentleman's Club]] — Elias Mercer's small occult club; attenuated descendants still in the Heights. *(Provisional.)*
- [[Hollowed Townspeople]] — the walking agency-loss casualties (Voss, Pete, Ellen, Carl + an unnamed class); walk home through the mill south gate.
