# Episode 7 — The Breather

Up: [[Episodes]]

**A pure town-side, Wesley-free bottle episode — a deliberate exhale that gives the PCs space to think and plan, and a low-stakes window for relationship maintenance.** No featured Rival. Wesley fingerprint level: **none.** Lightly sketched (design 1–2 ahead).

## The episode
By now the Wesley investigation is cooking in the PCs' minds; an episode that *doesn't* advance it lets them breathe and plan. Players will appreciate the exhale. Pick a small bottle-style thing (if [[Episode 4 — The Mirror]] used the Mirror, choose something else here):
- A haunted object at an estate sale
- A weird incident at [[Mercer Lake|the lake]]
- A misbehaving artifact in the school's storage room

**Townspeople are weird; the town has always been weird; Wesley isn't behind every weird thing.** That's the point of the breather.

## Disposition layer
Good for **incidental relationship maintenance** — low-key Time Points investments that lift disposition by 1 step without the weight of a confrontation:
- A quiet day catching up with [[Ricky Vance|Ricky]] at [[Marco's Diner|the diner]]
- Sitting with [[Marcy Hauer|Marcy]] in her grandmother's garden
- A small research thread with [[Eddie Park|Eddie]] that *isn't* about Wesley

The campaign rewards this kind of investment cumulatively. See [[The Disposition Tracker|The Negotiation System]].
