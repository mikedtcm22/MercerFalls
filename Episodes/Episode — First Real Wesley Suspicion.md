# Episode — First Real Wesley Suspicion

Up: [[Episodes]]

**The community-center pattern becomes hard to miss: a second Tinsmith brother dies, and a PC investigating finds [[Wesley's Sigils|Wesley's chalk]] nearby. Phase 3 of the [[The Pattern Recognition Arc]] begins.** No new featured Rival — the established Rivals' disposition states start coming into focus as a *pattern.* Wesley fingerprint level: **clear.** Lightly sketched (design 1–2 ahead).

## The cornerstone
Tied to the **first [[The Three Ritual Items|Weight]] attempt failure**: **Doug Tinsmith hollowed mid-August** (the attempt fails; Wesley aborts via sigil; no retrieval — see [[Wesley's Summer Calendar]]). Doug dies days later in a ladder fall.

The downstream manifestation is open:
- A **doppelgänger episode** — Doug's hollowed self acting strangely, neighbors confused about which version they're talking to before he dies
- Or a more explicit dungeon-leak

> [!danger] DM-only
> **The pattern is now hard to miss.** Doug was a community center volunteer. Mike was a community center volunteer. [[Henry Voss]] (the Hungry House) was at the center for grief support. PCs investigating Doug's death/walk-home find [[Wesley Crane|Wesley's]] chalk markings nearby. The faceup conversation might happen here or in [[Episode — The Breather]]. (The [[The Keystone Theft]] interlude may have already pulled the **click-2** transition earlier than the default [[Episode — Wesley Confirmed|Wesley Confirmed]] timing.)

## Disposition layer
If the PCs have built bonds with any Rivals by now, their reactions to Doug's death become disposition events: what they say to [[Marcy Hauer|Marcy]], whether they tell [[Ricky Vance|Ricky]] what they suspect, whether they bring [[Eddie Park|Eddie]] into the loop. **The campaign's first sustained payoff for early relationship work** — the PCs now have specific people to pull closer or push away. The PCs may notice Marcy, Ricky, Eddie, and [[The Fourth Recruit]] are all increasingly visible at the community center. See [[The Disposition Tracker|The Negotiation System]].
