# Episode — Storm-That-Doesn't-Move

Up: [[Episodes]]

**A storm that won't move sits over the spot where Mike Tinsmith drowned — residue spillover from Wesley's Floor-2 harvest. This is the bizarro-plot cornerstone where Wesley's name first enters the PCs' mouths.** Featured Rival: **TBD** (this slot originally belonged to Sam — now dissolved; see below). Wesley fingerprint level: **clear**.

## The monster
[[The Storm-That-Doesn't-Move]] over [[The Fishing Dock]]: a cloud fixed against the wind, rain in a ~40-ft circle, the water inside *wrong* — fish surface dead, people feel briefly hollowed before the [[The Reality Filter|Filter]] restores them. A first sustained look at **agency-loss as a force, not just a state** (see [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]]; a [[Cosmology Seeds|cosmology seed]]).

> [!danger] DM-only
> **The cornerstone.** [[Mike and Doug Tinsmith|Mike Tinsmith]] was hollowed in [[Wesley Crane]]'s late-July Floor-2 delve; the [[The Three Ritual Items|Gathered Dust]] harvested from his chamber is escaping back up through his death-site. Mike drowned days later in a fishing trip Wesley casually suggested. **Wesley fingerprint level: clear** — Mike was a community center volunteer, and the investigation surfaces it. The PCs may not connect it yet, but **Wesley's name is now in their mouths** as more than a friendly background figure. The pattern starts here. (See [[The Pattern Recognition Arc]], [[Wesley's Summer Calendar]].)

## Town investigation
Mike's death is officially a fishing accident — but a man who fished that stretch thirty years had no business missing the current. His brother **Doug** is grieving and spending more time at the community center; **Doug is next to be hollowed** in [[Episode — First Real Wesley Suspicion]].

Disperse the storm by **clearing the source-chamber on Floor 2** (the residue's origin) or a **localized cleansing at the dock** (which needs dungeon-delved materials anyway).
