# Episodes

Up: [[Embers of the Gathering Hollows — Overview]]

The session-by-session **foreground of play** — each a Monster-of-the-Week with town investigation and (usually) dungeon resolution, paced against [[Wesley's Summer Calendar]]. Episodes 1–2 are the most developed; later episodes are sketches that get prepped 1–2 ahead, so most carry `provisional` or `stub` status. The catalog is a **menu of options**, not a railroad: adapt to what the PCs are interested in. Each episode features a Rival whose [[The Negotiation System|disposition]] the PCs shape, cashing in at [[The Equinox Arc]]. See [[The Bizarro Plot]] and [[The Pattern Recognition Arc]] for the throughlines.
