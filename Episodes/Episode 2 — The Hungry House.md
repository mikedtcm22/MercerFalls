# Episode 2 — The Hungry House

Up: [[Episodes]]

**A house on Pine Street has begun *feeding* — pets, then a kid on a bike, then the mailman. The cause is a piece of dungeon-stone its hollowed owner carried home in 1991.** A pure town episode that introduces [[Earl]]'s pawn shop and plants the 1991-survivor thread. Featured Rival: [[Marcy Hauer]] (starting disposition 2 — Neutral). Wesley fingerprint level: **none**.

## The monster & cause
The house ([[The Hungry House]]) is becoming an annex of [[The Dungeon|the dungeon]]: a small chamber has formed in the basement and is growing. Pets vanish near the front yard; a kid on a bike; the mailman turns up two days later confused, missing a morning.

> [!danger] DM-only
> The owner, **[[Henry Voss]]**, is a 1991 mill survivor — hollowed for five years, shrugging through days at the [[The Sinclair Station|Sinclair station]] (see [[The 1991 Mill Incident]], [[Hollowing and Agency-Loss]]). After the incident he walked home with a piece of **dungeon-stone** in his lunchbox he doesn't remember picking up. The stone is slowly making the house dungeon-annex. Voss isn't lying when he says he doesn't remember — he genuinely doesn't.

## Town investigation
Neighbors, missing-pet posters, the mailman's account, Voss's hollowed affect. PCs may notice his slow drawl matches other townspeople they've met — the **first whisper that 1991 wasn't an isolated accident** (a [[Cosmology Seeds|cosmology seed]]; Filter-piercing Voss is hard at Level 1 and probably won't happen yet).

## Introducing Earl's pawn shop
The natural introduction point for [[Earl's Pawn Shop]] in [[The Districts|The Flats]]. Voss has pawned things on a slow drip for years. [[Earl]] has noticed *the pattern* — he doesn't use the word "hollowed," but he keeps a **private ledger** and effectively knows accurate counts of [[Hollowed Townspeople]]. Time Points invested in Earl can gradually surface the ledger as an investigative resource. Earl is the town's accidental, accurate witness.

## Marcy's connection & disposition
Marcy's late brother **Tommy** was friends with Henry Voss before 1991; she still checks on Voss (flowers on the anniversary, sitting with him). A PC following the Voss thread meets her there:
- **Connects through Tommy's memory, treats the visit as meaningful** → **3 (Sympathetic)**, or **4 (Connected)** with sustained effort
- **Brusque / treats her visits as suspicious** → **1 (Wary)**
- **Has the conversation but doesn't go deep** → stays at **2**, trending up
- **Doesn't engage her** → stays at **2**; Wesley works her from there

After this episode [[Wesley Crane]] begins counter-cultivating Marcy. See [[The Disposition Tracker|The Negotiation System]].

## Resolution
Pure town episode (the chamber is in the basement): **destroy the dungeon-stone** (closing the annex) or **extract it** (localized residue dispersal). Either works. The basement chamber holds a creature or two — small dungeon-natives like Floor 1.
